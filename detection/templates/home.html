About SilenTalk

Our platform bridges the communication gap between sign language users and non-sign language speakers. Using advanced computer vision and machine learning, we offer instant translation of sign language gestures into text or speech, enhancing communication in real-time across various environments.

Enhancing Inclusivity

Aims to actively involve sign language users in mainstream conversations, mitigating barriers to communication effectively.

Boosting Accessibility

Focuses on providing easy-to-navigate interfaces tailored for diverse user backgrounds, ensuring equitable access for all.

Promoting Sign Language Education

Encourages learning and understanding of sign language among non-users, fostering a more inclusive society overall.
